# Feature Backlog Planner

## Workflow

- Inspect project structure and docs.
- Do not change code.
- Propose small tasks.
- Prioritize by P0/P1/P2.
- Include risk, complexity, tests, browser verification, RLS/security and business concerns.
- Recommend the next best task.
